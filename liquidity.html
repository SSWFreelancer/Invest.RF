How it works

Selling

Buying

1

Pick an asset to sell

From your portfolio page, eligible assets can now be posted for sale. Click the “Buy / Sell” button to begin your request.
2

Name your price

Decide how much you want to sell.* Set your asking price and post your request to the market. *Right now, you can only request to sell your entire holding. We hope to enable partial sales soon.
3

Wait for a buyer

Once a buyer requests to buy your assets, you’ll be asked to approve the transaction. Once you do, we’ll contact the issuer* to finalize the transfer. *This step is legally required, we expect it to take 5-10 business days, but it can take longer.
4

Wait for issuer confirmation

Once the issuer approves, the ownership will be transferred to the buyer and funds will be sent to your bank account.
