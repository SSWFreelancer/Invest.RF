Getting started
It's never been easier to discover, invest and share in the success of businesses.

Discover businesses

It's simple and free for anyone in Europe to sign up. Once your investor profile is complete you'll be able to access all campaigns, ask entrepreneurs questions, request further information and invest.

Start investing

To invest, choose the amount and securely pay for your investment by debit card or bank transfer. It's simple to create a diversified portfolio of investments to spread your exposure to risk.

Share the success

Become a legal, ordinary shareholder when the round closes. Receive regular updates and engage with each business through Seedrs or offline. If the business does well you'll receive a return on your investment.
Learn more about becoming an investor
Types of equity
Invest in the equity of ambitious, growth-focused businesses and receive full voting shares with professional grade investor protections. There are 3 ways you can invest on Seedrs:

Equity

The simplest and most common way to invest and become a shareholder in a business. Starting at £/€100

Funds

Diversify across multiple businesses with a single investment, and become a shareholder in each. Starting at £/€100

Convertible

Invest in a business now and your investment will convert, at a discount to other investors, in the future. Starting at £/€10
