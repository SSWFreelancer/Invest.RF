Why become a startup investor
Angel investing — hunting for a unicorn — was previously available to only the wealthiest 3% of the US population.
With Republic, everyone can invest in private startups for a chance to earn a return.
Become an investor

Unicorns are startups that reach the valuation of over $1B. If you find and invest in one at an early stage, you could earn a significant return on investment. While no one can predict which companies will become unicorns, Republic gives you access to companies who have the potential to become one.
